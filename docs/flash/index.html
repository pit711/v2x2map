V2X2MAP ITS-G5 Receiver · Web Flasher

Flash the receiver right in your browser

Connect the ESP32-C5 to your computer via USB and click Connect & Flash. No install, no drivers: the receiver derives its ID automatically from its own hardware address and shows up over Bluetooth in the V2X2MAP app right after it reboots.

Firmware v0.2.4 · ESP32-C5
⚠️ Couldn’t start automatically — put the board into bootloader mode by hand:
  1. Seeed XIAO ESP32-C5: hold BOOT (B), tap RESET (R) once, then release BOOT
  2. Tick “already in bootloader mode” above
  3. Click Connect & Flash again and re-select the same port
Still no luck? The desktop flasher (macOS/Windows) resets the C5 automatically — use that instead.
