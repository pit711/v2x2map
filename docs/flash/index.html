V2X2MAP ITS-G5 Receiver · Web Flasher

Flash the receiver right in your browser

Connect the ESP32-C5 to your computer via USB and click Connect & Flash — the browser does the rest. No install, no drivers, no configuration: the receiver derives its ID automatically from its own hardware address and shows up over Bluetooth in the V2X2MAP app right after it reboots.

⚠️ This browser doesn’t support Web Serial. Please use Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge on a computer — or grab the desktop flasher. ⚠️ Serial-port access was blocked (HTTPS required). Firmware v0.2.4
Device not found? Put it into boot mode manually: 1. Hold the BOOT button  →  2. Tap RESET once (keep holding BOOT)  →  3. Release BOOT and click Connect & Flash again
Works in Chrome, Edge & Opera on a computer (Web Serial). Safari, Firefox and mobile devices aren’t supported — use the desktop flasher (macOS/Windows) there.

How it works

  1. Plug the ESP32-C5 receiver in with a USB cable
  2. Click Connect & Flash and pick the serial port in the browser dialog (e.g. “USB JTAG/serial debug unit”)
  3. Confirm Install — flashing takes about a minute
  4. Done! The receiver reboots → open the V2X2MAP app on your phone
